Test page for HVAC_Legos Class

  • The sources are represented by the green circles.  They have no input and have an STP output at 100m/s, 100000Pa and 300 K.
  • The heaters are represented by the red squares.  They double the temperature and double the flow, leaving the pressure the same.
  • The fans are represented by the blue square.  They double the pressure and halve the flow, leaving the temperature the same.
It's perfectly straightforward to add button for coolers and/or "decompressors"(?), or have any other type of HVAComponent that had an output that was an arbitrary function of its input.

Hooking up components with multiple inputs and outputs would be more complicated, but it can be done.
